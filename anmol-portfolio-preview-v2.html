About

Editor by craft.
Director of feeling.

I treat every cut like a decision the viewer will feel — even if they never notice it. From real-estate luxury reels to knowledge-center socials and product stories, I build edits that look expensive and move fast.

Tools I live in: Premiere Pro, After Effects, color, sound, and a ruthless timeline.

  • Vertical + landscape delivery from one story
  • Music-led pacing & caption-ready exports
  • Fast feedback loops, clear revisions

Process

From raw to ready.

  1. 01

    Brief

    Goals, references, platforms, aspect ratios, and the feeling you want people to leave with.

  2. 02

    Rough cut

    Structure, hooks, and story first. You’ll see the bones before the polish.

  3. 03

    Finish

    Motion, grade, sound, captions — then exports for every format you need.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Master files + platform-ready cuts. Optional thumbnails and cutdowns.

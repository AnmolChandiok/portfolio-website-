Drop your actual video files in this folder (real files, not Finder aliases!)
then point to them in js/config.js like:  src: "media/your-file.mp4"

Web-friendly export from Premiere/AE:
  H.264 .mp4, target ~5-10 Mbps, ideally under ~20 MB per clip.
  Hero loop clips can be much smaller (short, muted, 720p is fine).
